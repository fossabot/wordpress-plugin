== Installation ==

You can use the built in installer and upgrader, or you can install the plugin manually.

### Automatic Installation
1. Install directly from your Dashboard (Plugins > Add New). Just search for "Selz ecommerce".

### Manual Installation
1. Download the plugin zip file to your computer
2. Navigate to to the 'Plugins' page in the WordPress Dashboard
3. Select 'Add New > Upload > Choose File' and select the Selz zip file

Once the plugin has been activated you will have a Selz button available in your Visual editor. Click on the Selz ("S") button to insert your Selz product into your page or post. You will need to copy the short link from the bottom of the "Share" page on Selz where you normally share to Facebook or Twitter. 


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

### What is Selz?
Our mission is to make it incredibly simple for you to sell what you do and get paid. Selz requires no programming skills, it handles all aspects of selling and getting paid and it takes only moments to setup. And we're constantly adding new features to help creative entrepreneurs like you realise their potential. For more info visit the [Selz website](http://selz.com)

### How do I insert an item for sale into a post of page? 

1. Once you've installed and activated the plugin, within your editor click the Selz "S" button to show the Selz shortcode generator. 
2. Copy the short link from the bottom of the "Share item" page on Selz. If you're stuck, see the section below on how to get the link. Paste this into the "Item Link" section on the plugin editor.
3. Customize the position, colors and choose whether to display your item within an overlay or as a new tab.
4. You're done!
 
Check out the video on the description tab if you're still lost.

### How do I get the item link?

1. Sign up or sign in to Selz
2. If you already have an item on Selz, hover on the item you want to add and click 'Share'. If you have just signed up, you'll need to add a new item.
3. Once you're done adding an item or you've clicked 'Share', you'll see the 'Share item' page. At the bottom there is a link that starts with 'http://selz.co', copy that or click 'Copy link'.
4. Paste this into the WordPress dialog where it asks for the item link.

### Will Selz work with my theme?
Yes, Selz will work with any theme, you can customise your button or widget colours to make it match nicely your website. 

### How do I get paid? 
Depending where you are we pay directly into your bank account or to your Paypal account. Sellers receive payouts every Friday for any sales received in the previous week.

When you make a sale we securely process the buyer's card payment. When we transfer the money across to your account Selz takes it's processing fees.

### How much does Selz cost?
There are no setup fees or monthly fees. You only pay when you make a sale. We have a simple flat fee of 5%+ 25c per sale.
